EcoSafe-AI Forest Fire Detection

Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. This policy explains how the EcoSafe-AI mobile app and backend API collect, use, store, and protect your information.

Last updated: May 30, 2026  ·  Version 1.0

1 Introduction

EcoSafe-AI ("we", "our", "the App") is a forest fire detection application that uses your device camera and location services to analyze images and report potential fire incidents. By using EcoSafe-AI, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

App Purpose Early forest fire detection and emergency reporting
Platform Android mobile app + EcoSafe-AI backend API
Data Controller EcoSafe-AI Project Team

2 Information We Collect

We collect only the data necessary to provide fire detection and incident reporting:

Data Type How It Is Collected Purpose
Camera Images When you capture or upload a photo for analysis AI-based fire detection
GPS Location Device location services (with your permission) Pinpoint fire incident location on map
Detection Results Generated by the AI model after image analysis Display results, history, and alerts
Timestamps Automatically recorded at detection time Incident history and filtering
Device Identifier Generic user ID ("android_user") sent with requests Associate reports with app sessions

We do not require account registration, email, phone number, or personal identity information to use the core features of the app.

3 How We Use Your Information

  • Process images through our TensorFlow Lite fire detection model
  • Store fire incident records with location and confidence scores
  • Display detection history and map locations within the app
  • Enable emergency contact and location sharing features you initiate
  • Improve reliability of the detection and reporting system

4 Data Storage & Retention

Your data may be stored in the following locations:

  • On your device: Detection history is saved locally in an SQLite database (incidents.db) including image paths, coordinates, and results.
  • On the backend server: Fire incidents and safe detection reports are stored in the EcoSafe-AI server database when the app is connected to the network.

You can delete local history from the History screen in the app. Backend records are retained for operational and safety purposes unless deleted by an administrator.

5 Permissions Required

The Android app requests the following permissions:

  • Camera — to capture forest area photos for analysis
  • Location (Fine & Coarse) — to attach GPS coordinates to reports
  • Internet — to communicate with the EcoSafe-AI backend API
  • Storage — to select images from your gallery
  • Phone (Dial) — to initiate emergency calls (1122, 1084, 15) when you tap emergency buttons; the app does not make calls automatically

You may deny location permission and still use detection, but incident coordinates may be unavailable or less accurate.

6 Third-Party Services

EcoSafe-AI integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Google Maps SDK — displays incident locations on a map. Google's Privacy Policy applies: policies.google.com/privacy
  • Google Play Services (Location) — provides device GPS coordinates

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

7 Data Sharing

We do not share your data except in these cases:

  • When you choose to share: The "Share Report" feature lets you manually send detection details via other apps (e.g., messaging apps).
  • Emergency services: When you tap emergency call buttons, your phone's dialer opens — we do not transmit data directly to authorities.
  • Legal requirements: If required by applicable law or valid government request.

8 Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data, including local device storage and server-side database access controls. However, no method of transmission over a network is 100% secure. During development, the app may connect to a local backend over HTTP — for production deployment, HTTPS encryption is strongly recommended.

9 Your Rights & Choices

  • Revoke permissions: Disable camera or location access in your device Settings at any time
  • Delete local history: Use the Clear All or swipe-to-delete options in the History screen
  • Opt out of location: Deny location permission; detection will still work without GPS
  • Request data deletion: Contact us to request removal of backend-stored incident records

10 Children's Privacy

EcoSafe-AI is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

11 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
